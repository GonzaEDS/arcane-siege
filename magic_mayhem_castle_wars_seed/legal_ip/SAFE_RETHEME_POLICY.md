# Safe Retheme Policy

This project seed is for a mechanically compatible, original presentation. It is not legal advice.

## Use

- Functional mechanics, numeric balancing, original code, original UI, original names for the shipped game.
- Public-domain mythological creature concepts.
- Original placeholder or generated art that does not copy specific official assets.

## Avoid

- Official Magic & Mayhem or Castle Wars title, logos, screenshots, music, UI art, sprites, card images, models, videos, manual scans, or copied prose.
- Marketing the game as an official adaptation.
- Copying recognizable non-functional expression from either game.

## Internal references

It is acceptable for internal development data to include `source_name` fields for QA mapping. Do not expose these as the primary public branding unless rights are clarified.

## Production naming

Recommended public working title: **Arcane Siege: Portmanteau Duels**.
