# Magic & Mayhem Retheme Guide

## What to borrow as inspiration

Use the following as thematic pillars:

- Wizard duel structure.
- Summoned creatures as living spells.
- Law / Neutral / Chaos talismans as the three resource colors.
- Ingredients, artifacts, and strange mythic props as card identities.
- Places of Power as the production engine.
- Avalon, Greece, and Albion as mood boards: Celtic woods, Greek ruins, Arthurian castles.
- Stop-motion/clay creature energy translated into original hand-made/idol-like card frames.

## What not to borrow literally

Do not ship under the Magic & Mayhem name. Do not copy official art, logos, music, videos, UI, text passages, sprites, models, screenshots, or exact story scenes.

## Presentation language

Original resource mapping:

- Bricks → Law Mana
- Crystals → Neutral Mana
- Swords/Weapons → Chaos Mana
- Builders → Law Circles
- Mages → Neutral Circles
- Soldiers/Recruits → Chaos Circles
- Castle → Citadel
- Wall → Ward
- Deck → Portmanteau
- Play card → Cast
- Discard → Return to Portmanteau

## Card naming rules

- Use mythic/common-public-domain names whenever possible: troll, faun, unicorn, skeleton, basilisk, dragon, manticore, centaur.
- Specific Magic & Mayhem names may remain internal reference tags only until rights are clarified.
- Preserve source card names in QA/debug mode only.
