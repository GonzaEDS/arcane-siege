# Balance and Adaptation Notes

## First principle

No balance changes in phase 1. The clone baseline is the control group.

## Why exact mechanics first

The source game is already a known playable system. Any thematic adaptation must be compared against a working baseline; otherwise it will be impossible to tell whether problems are implementation bugs, balance issues, or theme mismatch.

## Later adaptation candidates

Only after exact baseline tests pass:

- Ingredient drafting: before match, players choose a small set of ingredients that bias deck generation.
- Places of Power: alternate mode where players can invest mana to permanently shift production.
- Creature allegiance: Law/Neutral/Chaos bonuses when cards are chained in same color.
- Realm skins: Avalon/Greece/Albion boards with identical mechanics but different card art prompts.

## Metrics to collect

- Average turns per game.
- Win rate by first player.
- Frequency of dead hands.
- Frequency of no-affordable-card turns.
- Most-played and least-played cards.
- Win rate by deck preset.
